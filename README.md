# ‚òï Coffee Notes
**A cozy, handwritten-style productivity tool for your browser.**

Coffee Notes is a minimalist Chrome Extension designed for those who love the aesthetic of a local coffee shop and the feel of a handwritten checklist. Built with a "Paper & Ink" philosophy, it helps you stay focused on your tasks without the clutter of complex apps.

![Coffee Notes Preview](icon.png)

## ‚ú® Features
* **Handwritten Aesthetic:** Uses the *Gochi Hand* font for a human touch.
* **Dynamic Themes:** Seamlessly switch between **Light (Old Paper)** and **Dark (Espresso)** modes.
* **Persistent Memory:** Your tasks are saved automatically and sync across your Google account.
* **Strike-through Logic:** Click a task to cross it off your list.
* **Data Portability:** Download your list as a `.txt` file at any time.
* **Minimalist UI:** No ads, no tracking, just your notes.

## üöÄ How to Install (Manual Method)
Since this extension is not currently on the Chrome Web Store, you can install it manually in less than a minute:

1.  **Download the Project:**
    * Click the green **Code** button at the top of this page.
    * Select **Download ZIP**.
    * Extract (unzip) the file to a folder on your computer.

2.  **Open Chrome Extensions:**
    * Open Google Chrome.
    * In the address bar, type `chrome://extensions/` and hit Enter.

3.  **Enable Developer Mode:**
    * In the top right corner, toggle the **Developer mode** switch to **ON**.

4.  **Load the Extension:**
    * Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top left.
    * Select the folder you just unzipped (the one containing `manifest.json`).

5.  **Pin for Easy Access:**
    * Click the **Puzzle Piece icon** in your Chrome toolbar.
    * Find **Coffee Notes** and click the **Pin icon** to keep it visible.
